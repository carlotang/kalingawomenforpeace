About

This capstone project, entitled, "Bobbobai ni Kalinga para ad Kapya: Documentation of the Roles of Women in Bodong" aims to inform the public of the various roles that women play in Bodong, which is an indigenous dispute settlement and peacebuilding mechanism between Kalinga tribes. It also seeks to feature stories about women's leadership in grassroots conflict resolution, with the end view of inspiring young women and girls belonging to indigenous cultural communities to assert themselves and take an active role in building peaceful and harmonious communities.
